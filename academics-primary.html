Primary Level: Building a Strong Foundation

The Primary Level at Phoenix is a vibrant learning journey where young minds flourish. We ignite a passion for learning and equip students with the essential skills they need to thrive. Our curriculum emphasizes core subjects like language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies, while fostering creativity, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities.

Key Features

  • Focus on core subjects: language arts, mathematics, science, and social studies.
  • Integration of technology to enhance learning and develop digital literacy.
  • Nurturing creativity, imagination, and problem-solving skills.
  • Engaging extra-curricular activities for well-rounded development.
