Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q: What is the age criteria for admission to Pre-Primary?

A: Children between the ages of 2.5 and 3 years are eligible for admission to our Pre-Primary program.

Q: What is the admission process for international students?

A: We welcome applications from international students! For details about the specific requirements and application procedures, please refer to our Admission Information page.

Q: Does the school offer scholarships?

A: Yes, Phoenix School is committed to supporting deserving students. We offer a limited number of scholarships based on academic merit and financial need. To learn more about scholarship opportunities and eligibility criteria, please contact our admissions office: admissions@phoenixschool.edu

Q: What are the deadlines for submitting applications?

A: Application deadlines vary depending on the grade level. Please refer to our Admission Information page for the most up-to-date deadlines.

Q: Can I visit the school campus before applying?

A: Absolutely! We encourage prospective students and their families to schedule a campus tour. Please contact our admissions office at info@phoenixschool.edu to schedule a visit.
