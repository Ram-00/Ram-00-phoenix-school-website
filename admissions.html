A Smooth Journey to Joining Our School Family

We understand that choosing the right school is an important decision. Our admissions process is designed to be transparent and informative, making it easier for you and your child to navigate each step.

  • Explore and Discover: Schedule a personalized school tour to experience our vibrant learning environment firsthand.
    Contact us to schedule a tour today!
  • Application Made Easy: Submit the online application form along with required documents. We'll guide you through the process every step of the way.
  • Entrance Assessment (if applicable): Students may be required to take an age-appropriate entrance assessment to evaluate their academic readiness.
  • Selection and Interview: Shortlisted candidates will be invited for an interview with our welcoming admissions committee.
  • Timely Decision: You will be notified of the admissions decision within a specified timeframe, allowing you to plan for the future with confidence.
