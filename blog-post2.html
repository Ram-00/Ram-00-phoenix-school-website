Developing Critical Thinking Skills in Students

Critical thinking is an essential skill that empowers students to analyze information, solve problems effectively, and make informed decisions.

At Phoenix, we employ various strategies to foster critical thinking, including:

  • Inquiry-based learning
  • Project-based learning
  • Debates and discussions
  • Problem-solving activities

These methods encourage students to question assumptions, evaluate evidence, and develop their own perspectives.
