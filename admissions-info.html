Admission Information

We welcome applications from students who are eager to learn and grow in a nurturing and challenging environment. We believe in selecting students who are curious, motivated, demonstrate a strong work ethic, and possess a positive attitude.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Age eligibility as per the relevant academic level.
  • Meet the academic requirements for the desired grade (previous school reports, standardized test scores, if applicable).

Application Process & Important Dates:

For detailed information about the application process, required documents, application deadlines, and other important dates, please refer to our Admission Process page.

Financial Aid (Optional):

Phoenix is committed to making a quality education accessible to all deserving students. We offer various financial aid options to help ease the financial burden. Learn more about our financial aid programs and eligibility criteria on our dedicated financial aid page (link to financial aid page).
