Middle & Secondary Level: Charting the Course to Success

The Middle and Secondary Levels at Phoenix empower students to become independent learners and critical thinkers. Our rigorous curriculum fosters a deep understanding of core subjects while nurturing a passion for exploration and discovery. We offer a diverse range of subjects, including languages, mathematics, science, social studies, arts, and physical education, to cater to individual strengths and aspirations.

Key Features

  • Development of critical thinking, problem-solving, and research skills.
  • Exploration of passions through electives and extracurricular activities.
  • Preparation for higher education with academic guidance.
  • Cultivating leadership skills and responsible citizenship.
